About

At Clinton Senior High School, I work as an IT Assistant, where I provide technical support and troubleshooting for various devices and software applications. I am an honors student and a member of the National Honor Society, and I am taking AP classes related to these fields. I have also participated in the FIRST Robotics program, where I designed and programmed robots using Java and Onshape. I am motivated to learn more about the latest technologies and innovations, and to apply them to real-world problems and challenges.
